Apple Pie

I would always make applie pie as a child with my grandparents so now I continue the tradition with everyone else so we can all enjoy apple pie.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pie Crust
  • 6 Cups of Thinly Sliced Apples
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 3/4 Cup of Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp Ground Nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup Raisins or Chopped Walnuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare and roll out the pie crust. Line a 9-inch pie plate with half of the pie crust
  2. If desired, sprinkle apples with lemon juice. In a large mixing bowl stir together sugar, flour, Cinnamon and nutmeg. Add apple slices and, if desired, raisins or walnuts. Gently toss till coated.
  3. Transfer apple micture to the pastry-lined pie plate. Trim pastry to edge of pie palte. Cut slits in remaining pastry; place on filling and seal. Crimp edge as desired.
  4. To prevent overbrowning, cover edge of pie with foil. Bake in a 375° oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes more or till top is golden. Cool on a wire rack.
