How to flip a pancake

First you have to get your trusty spatula from the drawer. Make sure it is the best because you need absolute precision if you want to flip your pancake.

Now that you have your spatula, carefully slide it underneath your pancake. Once the pancake is sitting atop your spatula lift it upwards and quickly rotate it so that the pancake falls back onto the stovetop.

Ingredients for Pancakes

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Steps to Make Pancakes

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Pour batter onto the pan.
  3. Flip the pancake when bubbles appear.
